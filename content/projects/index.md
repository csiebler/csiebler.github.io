## [modelavailability.com](https://modelavailability.com)

As an AI developer, figuring out which models are available in which regions across cloud providers can be tedious. Model Availability makes this easy by allowing to search and filter 300+ models across Azure, AWS, and GCP by region, deployment type, and pricing. Shows models from OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta, Mistral, DeepSeek, Google, and more. Data is updated daily. Also features tools, such as an [Azure PTU Calculator](https://modelavailability.com/tools/azure-ptu-calculator) and detailed model overviews.
