Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

Your privacy is fundamental to Ovuly. This policy explains what data the Ovuly mobile application ("App") handles, where it is stored, and your rights. In short: your health data never leaves your device, and we have no way to access it.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. There is no account system, no cloud sync, no analytics SDK, and no advertising tracker in the App. We have zero access to your health information.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage frameworks. It never leaves your device through the App.

Data typePurpose
Period & spotting logsCycle tracking and predictions
Symptoms & moodPattern recognition and daily logging
Journal notesPersonal notes you choose to record
Cycle settings (cycle length, period length)Prediction accuracy
Notification preferencesScheduling local reminders

3. Apple Health (HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, the App may read and write the following HealthKit data types:

Data typeReadWritePurpose
Menstrual flowYesYesSync period logs
SpottingYesYesSync spotting logs
Wrist temperatureYesNoPhase predictions
Heart rate variability (HRV)YesNoCycle insights
Resting heart rateYesNoCycle insights
Sleep analysisYesNoWellness correlations

All HealthKit data stays on your device and is governed by Apple's HealthKit privacy protections. You can grant or revoke access at any time in Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices.

We never access HealthKit data in the background, and we never transfer HealthKit data off your device or to any third party.

4. Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. Apple's privacy policy governs the transaction data they handle.

5. Notifications

The App may send local notifications (period reminders, daily log reminders, fertile window alerts) if you enable them. These notifications are scheduled on-device and do not involve any external push notification service or server.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. The only external communication is with Apple's StoreKit framework for subscription management.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Delete individual entries within the App at any time.
  • Delete all App data by uninstalling the App from your device.
  • Revoke HealthKit access in your device's Health settings.

We cannot delete your data for you because we do not have access to it.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child is using the App inappropriately, please contact us.

9. Security

Your data benefits from the security features built into iOS, including device encryption and biometric lock. Since data is stored only on your device and never transmitted, there is no server-side attack surface.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

11. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store your personal data, traditional data-subject rights (access, portability, erasure) are already satisfied — your data is entirely in your possession on your device. If you have questions about exercising your rights, contact us.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@ovuly.com.
